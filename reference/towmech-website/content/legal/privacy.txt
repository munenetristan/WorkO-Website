We collect job-related data such as:
- Job requests

- Assigned provider/customer details

1.5 Device & Technical Information
We may collect:
- Device model and OS version

- IP address

1.6 Communication & Support
We may store:

- Support tickets and communication history
- Reports, disputes, and complaint logs
